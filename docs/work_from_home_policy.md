# Work From Home Policy

## Purpose

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. supports a flexible hybrid work model that enables employees to work remotely while maintaining productivity, collaboration, and business continuity. This policy outlines the guidelines, expectations, and responsibilities for employees working from home.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all eligible employees whose roles permit remote work based on business requirements, project needs, and manager approval.

---

## Hybrid Work Model

The company follows a hybrid work approach where employees may work:

* From the office
* From home
* A combination of both, as determined by departmental requirements

Managers may establish team-specific schedules to support collaboration and project delivery.

---

## Working Hours

Employees working remotely are expected to:

* Follow standard working hours of **9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST**
* Be available during core business hours
* Attend scheduled meetings and discussions
* Maintain regular communication with team members and managers

---

## Availability and Communication

Employees working from home must:

* Remain reachable through approved communication channels
* Respond to work-related messages within a reasonable timeframe
* Keep their work status updated on communication platforms
* Notify their manager when unavailable during working hours

Approved communication tools include:

* Microsoft Teams
* Slack
* Company Email
* Project Management Platforms

---

## Productivity Expectations

Employees are expected to:

* Complete assigned tasks on time
* Meet project deadlines and deliverables
* Maintain the same level of performance expected in an office environment
* Participate actively in team meetings and collaborative activities

Performance will be evaluated based on outcomes and deliverables rather than location.

---

## Home Workspace Requirements

Employees should maintain:

* A reliable internet connection
* A secure and distraction-free work environment
* Access to company-approved devices and software
* Appropriate measures to protect company information

---

## Information Security

While working remotely, employees must:

* Use company-approved devices whenever possible
* Protect confidential information
* Use strong passwords and multi-factor authentication
* Connect through approved VPN services when required
* Avoid sharing company data with unauthorized individuals

Any security incident must be reported immediately to the IT team.

---

## Attendance and Reporting

Employees must:

* Record attendance through the designated attendance system
* Inform their reporting manager about planned absences
* Follow company leave procedures when taking time off

Working from home is not considered a substitute for approved leave.

---

## Equipment and Support

The company may provide approved work equipment such as:

* Laptops
* Monitors
* Keyboards and accessories
* Software licenses
* VPN access

Employees are responsible for safeguarding company-issued assets.

---

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers are responsible for:

* Monitoring team productivity
* Supporting employee well-being
* Ensuring effective communication
* Managing project deliverables
* Coordinating hybrid work schedules

---

## Policy Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in the revocation of remote work privileges or other corrective actions as deemed appropriate by management.

---

## Policy Review

This policy may be updated periodically based on organizational requirements, operational needs, and evolving workplace practices.
