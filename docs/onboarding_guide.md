# Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

We are excited to welcome you to Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. This guide will help you complete your onboarding process, understand company expectations, and gain access to the tools and resources required for your role.

---

## First-Day Checklist

### Human Resources

* Complete employee documentation and verification.
* Submit identification and banking details if not already provided.
* Review and acknowledge company policies.
* Verify emergency contact information.
* Complete onboarding forms provided by HR.

### IT Setup

* Receive company-issued laptop and accessories.
* Activate company email account.
* Configure multi-factor authentication (MFA).
* Set up VPN access if required.
* Verify access to required software and development tools.

### Team Introduction

* Meet your reporting manager.
* Meet team members and project stakeholders.
* Join relevant communication channels.
* Review team structure and responsibilities.

---

## First Week Objectives

Employees are encouraged to:

* Complete all onboarding activities.
* Review the Employee Handbook.
* Understand department goals and responsibilities.
* Attend scheduled onboarding sessions.
* Become familiar with company systems and workflows.
* Meet key team members and stakeholders.

---

## Learning Resources

Feku Tech Solutions encourages continuous learning through:

* Internal training sessions
* Technical workshops
* Certification programs
* Knowledge-sharing sessions
* Mentorship opportunities

Recommended learning paths are provided by managers based on role requirements.

## Company Policies to Review

All employees should carefully review:

* Employee Handbook
* Code of Conduct
* Leave Policy
* Attendance Policy
* Work From Home Policy
* Information Security Guidelines
* Benefits Policy

## Probation Period

New employees may be subject to a probation period as defined in their employment agreement.

During this period:

* Performance and cultural fit are evaluated.
* Regular feedback sessions may be conducted.
* Training and support will be provided to ensure successful integration.
