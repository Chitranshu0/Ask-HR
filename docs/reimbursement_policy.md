# Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. reimburses employees for legitimate business-related expenses incurred while performing authorized company duties. This policy outlines eligible expenses, approval requirements, submission procedures, and reimbursement timelines.

---

## Eligibility

* All full-time employees are eligible for reimbursement of approved business expenses.
* Contract employees may be eligible based on contractual agreements.
* Expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and directly related to company business.

---

## Eligible Expense Categories

### Travel Expenses

Employees may claim reimbursement for:

* Flight tickets (economy class)
* Train travel
* Local transportation (cab, metro, bus)
* Airport transfers
* Business-related fuel expenses

### Accommodation

Approved accommodation expenses during official business travel may be reimbursed subject to manager approval and company guidelines.

### Meals and Food

Reasonable meal expenses incurred during approved business travel or client meetings may be eligible for reimbursement.

### Internet and Communication

Employees working remotely may claim approved internet or communication expenses when authorized by management.

### Training and Certification

The company may reimburse:

* Professional certifications
* Technical training programs
* Industry workshops
* Approved learning resources

Prior approval is required before enrollment.

### Office and Business Expenses

Eligible expenses include:

* Printing and documentation
* Business-related software subscriptions
* Approved office supplies
* Project-specific purchases

---

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses are generally not eligible for reimbursement:

* Personal travel
* Personal meals
* Traffic fines and penalties
* Entertainment expenses without business justification
* Personal equipment purchases without approval
* Expenses lacking supporting documentation

---

## Approval Requirements

| Expense Type     | Approval Authority                |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Up to ₹5,000     | Reporting Manager                 |
| ₹5,001 - ₹25,000 | Department Head                   |
| Above ₹25,000    | Department Head + Finance Manager |

Pre-approval may be required for significant expenses.

---

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Collect Supporting Documents

Employees must retain:

* Original invoices
* Receipts
* Travel tickets
* Payment confirmations

### Step 2: Submit Expense Claim

Expense claims should include:

* Expense date
* Expense category
* Business purpose
* Amount claimed
* Supporting documentation

### Step 3: Manager Review

The reporting manager verifies the business justification and approves or rejects the claim.

### Step 4: Finance Processing

Approved claims are reviewed by the Finance Department for final processing.

---

## Reimbursement Timeline

* Claims should be submitted within **30 days** of the expense date.
* Approved reimbursements are generally processed within **15 business days**.
* Delayed submissions may require additional approval.

---

## Fraudulent Claims

Submitting false, misleading, or altered expense claims is considered a serious policy violation and may result in disciplinary action, including termination of employment.

---

## Record Keeping

Employees should maintain copies of submitted documentation for future reference and audit purposes.

The Finance Department reserves the right to request additional supporting information when necessary.

---

## Exceptions

Any exception to this policy requires written approval from the Finance Manager and Human Resources Department.
