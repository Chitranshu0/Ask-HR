# Benefits Policy

## Purpose

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is committed to supporting employee well-being, financial security, and professional growth through a comprehensive benefits program. This policy outlines the benefits available to eligible employees.

## Eligibility

* All full-time employees are eligible for company-sponsored benefits.
* Certain benefits may be subject to employment duration, role, or company policies.
* Contractual and internship positions may have separate benefit structures.

## Health Insurance

* Employees are covered under the company's group health insurance plan.
* Coverage includes hospitalization expenses, medical treatments, and emergency care as per the insurer's terms.
* Eligible dependents may be included based on the selected insurance plan.

## Provident Fund (PF)

* The company contributes to the Employee Provident Fund (EPF) in accordance with applicable regulations.
* Employee contributions are deducted from monthly salary as per statutory requirements.
* PF statements and account details can be accessed through the official EPFO portal.

## Performance Bonuses

* Employees may be eligible for annual performance bonuses based on:

  * Individual performance
  * Team performance
  * Company performance
* Bonus amounts are determined during the annual performance review cycle and are not guaranteed.

## Paid Leave Benefits

Employees are entitled to the following annual leave benefits:

* Casual Leave (CL)
* Sick Leave (SL)
* Earned Leave (EL)

Leave eligibility and usage are governed by the company's Leave Policy.

## Learning & Development

The company encourages continuous learning through:

* Online certification support
* Technical workshops
* Internal training programs
* Professional development initiatives

Manager approval may be required for sponsored learning programs.

## Employee Recognition

Outstanding contributions may be recognized through:

* Spot awards
* Performance awards
* Employee appreciation programs
* Project excellence recognition

## Employee Assistance

Employees may seek support from HR regarding workplace concerns, career development, and benefit-related queries.

## Policy Review

The company reserves the right to modify, update, or discontinue benefits based on business requirements and applicable regulations.

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Company:** Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
