# FAQs

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1. How do I apply for leave?

Employees can submit leave requests through the company's leave management system. All leave requests must be approved by the reporting manager before they are considered valid.

---

### 2. How can I check my leave balance?

You can view your available Casual Leave (CL), Sick Leave (SL), and Earned Leave (EL) through the employee portal or by contacting the HR department.

---

### 3. Who is my reporting manager?

Your reporting manager information is available in the employee directory and employee profile records maintained by the company.

---

### 4. What are the standard working hours?

The standard working schedule is:

* Monday to Friday
* 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST

Employees are expected to complete 8 working hours per day excluding breaks.

---

### 5. Does the company support remote work?

Yes. Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. follows a hybrid work model. Remote work arrangements may be approved based on project requirements and manager approval.

---

### 6. What types of leave are available?

Employees are eligible for:

* Casual Leave (CL)
* Sick Leave (SL)
* Earned Leave (EL)

Additional leave categories may be available according to company policy and applicable regulations.

---

### 7. How do I update my personal information?

Employees can request updates to personal information such as address, phone number, emergency contacts, and banking details through HR.

---

### 8. How do I access health insurance information?

Health insurance details, coverage information, and claim procedures can be obtained from the HR department or the benefits documentation provided during onboarding.

---

### 9. What should I do if I forget to mark attendance?

Inform your reporting manager and HR as soon as possible. Attendance corrections may be processed after verification.

---

### 10. How are performance reviews conducted?

Performance evaluations are conducted periodically and consider:

* Technical contributions
* Project performance
* Team collaboration
* Professional development
* Organizational impact

---

### 11. How can I request reimbursement?

Eligible expenses should be submitted through the reimbursement process along with valid supporting documents. Approval from the reporting manager may be required.

---

### 12. How do I report a workplace concern?

Employees are encouraged to contact HR or management regarding workplace concerns, policy violations, or professional conduct issues. Reports will be handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

### 13. What learning opportunities are available?

The company supports continuous learning through:

* Technical training programs
* Internal workshops
* Certification support
* Knowledge-sharing sessions
* Mentorship opportunities

---

### 14. What should I do if I face technical issues with company systems?

Employees should contact the IT support team or raise a support request through the designated internal support channels.

---

### 15. Who can I contact for HR-related assistance?

For any HR-related questions, employees can contact:

**Human Resources Department**
Email: [hr@fekutech.com](mailto:hr@fekutech.com)

The HR team is available to assist with leave management, attendance, benefits, policies, onboarding, and employee support.
