# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The Code of Conduct defines the professional standards, ethical principles, and workplace expectations for all employees of Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Every employee is expected to uphold these standards while representing the company, interacting with colleagues, clients, partners, and handling company resources.

## Professional Conduct

Employees are expected to:

* Act with honesty, integrity, and professionalism.
* Treat colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect.
* Maintain a positive and collaborative work environment.
* Communicate professionally in all verbal and written interactions.
* Take ownership of assigned responsibilities and deliver work to the best of their abilities.

## Workplace Respect

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is committed to maintaining an inclusive and respectful workplace.

Employees must:

* Respect diversity and individual differences.
* Avoid discrimination, harassment, bullying, or inappropriate behavior.
* Foster teamwork and mutual respect across all departments.

## Confidentiality

Employees must protect confidential company information, including:

* Client data
* Business strategies
* Financial information
* Product roadmaps
* Employee records
* Proprietary source code and technical assets

Confidential information must not be shared without proper authorization.

## Use of Company Resources

Employees are expected to use company resources responsibly, including:

* Laptops and devices
* Software licenses
* Cloud infrastructure
* Communication platforms
* Internal systems and databases

Company resources should be used primarily for business purposes.

## Conflict of Interest

Employees must avoid situations that could create conflicts between personal interests and company interests. Any potential conflict should be disclosed to management or HR immediately.

## Information Security

Employees must:

* Follow company security policies.
* Use strong passwords and secure authentication methods.
* Protect company systems from unauthorized access.
* Report security incidents promptly.

## Compliance

Employees must comply with all applicable company policies, legal requirements, and regulatory obligations.

## Reporting Concerns

Employees are encouraged to report unethical behavior, policy violations, security concerns, or workplace misconduct to HR or management without fear of retaliation.

## Disciplinary Action

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, suspension, or termination of employment depending on the severity of the violation.

## Acknowledgement

All employees are expected to read, understand, and comply with this Code of Conduct throughout their employment with Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Company:** Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
