## Launch of AskHR Initiative

**Date:** February 10, 2026

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. officially announced the development of **AskHR**, an Agentic AI-powered employee support platform.

The platform aims to:

* Provide instant HR policy assistance.
* Automate leave management workflows.
* Improve employee self-service capabilities.
* Reduce manual HR operational workload.
* Serve as the company's internal AI-powered workplace assistant.

The project is being developed collaboratively by the AI, GenAI, Backend, and HR teams.
