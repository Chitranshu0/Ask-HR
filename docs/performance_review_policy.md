# Performance Review Policy

## Purpose

The Performance Review Policy establishes a structured framework for evaluating employee performance, recognizing achievements, identifying development opportunities, and supporting career growth at Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

The objective of the performance management process is to encourage continuous improvement, align individual goals with business objectives, and ensure fair and transparent performance evaluations.

---

## Review Cycle

Performance reviews are conducted annually for all eligible employees.

### Review Period

* Review Cycle: January 1 – December 31
* Annual Review Discussions: January – February
* Performance Outcomes Communication: February

Managers may conduct additional quarterly or mid-year feedback sessions to support employee development.

---

## Eligibility

The performance review process applies to:

* Full-Time Employees
* Employees who have completed at least 6 months of service before the review cycle

Interns and contractual employees may be evaluated under separate assessment frameworks.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Performance evaluations consider multiple dimensions, including:

### Technical Excellence

* Quality of work
* Technical competency
* Problem-solving ability
* Innovation and continuous improvement

### Project Contributions

* Delivery of assigned tasks
* Meeting deadlines
* Ownership and accountability
* Impact on project success

### Collaboration

* Teamwork
* Communication skills
* Cross-functional cooperation
* Knowledge sharing

### Professional Growth

* Learning initiatives
* Certifications and training
* Adaptability to new technologies
* Leadership potential

### Organizational Impact

* Contribution to company objectives
* Customer satisfaction
* Process improvements
* Business value delivered

---

## Rating Scale

Employees are evaluated using the following five-point scale:

| Rating | Description          |
| ------ | -------------------- |
| 5      | Outstanding          |
| 4      | Exceeds Expectations |
| 3      | Meets Expectations   |
| 2      | Needs Improvement    |
| 1      | Unsatisfactory       |

### Rating Definitions

#### 5 - Outstanding

Consistently delivers exceptional results, demonstrates leadership, and significantly exceeds role expectations.

#### 4 - Exceeds Expectations

Frequently performs above expected standards and makes strong contributions to team and organizational goals.

#### 3 - Meets Expectations

Successfully fulfills job responsibilities and consistently meets performance requirements.

#### 2 - Needs Improvement

Performance falls below expected standards in certain areas and requires focused development.

#### 1 - Unsatisfactory

Performance consistently fails to meet role expectations and requires immediate corrective action.

---

## Review Process

### Self-Assessment

Employees may complete a self-evaluation outlining:

* Key accomplishments
* Challenges faced
* Learning achievements
* Career aspirations

### Manager Assessment

Managers evaluate employee performance based on:

* Goals and objectives
* Project outcomes
* Behavioral competencies
* Team contributions

### Performance Discussion

Managers conduct one-on-one review meetings to discuss:

* Achievements
* Areas for improvement
* Development plans
* Future goals

---

## Promotions and Career Growth

Promotion decisions may consider:

* Sustained high performance
* Technical expertise
* Leadership capabilities
* Business impact
* Organizational requirements

A strong performance rating does not automatically guarantee promotion.

---

## Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

Employees receiving low performance ratings may be enrolled in a Performance Improvement Plan.

The plan may include:

* Clearly defined improvement goals
* Regular progress reviews
* Coaching and mentoring support
* Specific timelines for improvement

---

## Recognition and Rewards

Performance outcomes may influence:

* Annual bonuses
* Salary revisions
* Promotions
* Recognition awards
* Leadership opportunities

Reward decisions are based on company performance, budget availability, and individual contributions.

---

## Confidentiality

Performance reviews are confidential and accessible only to:

* The employee
* Reporting manager
* Human Resources
* Authorized leadership personnel

---

## Policy Review

The company reserves the right to update this policy to align with organizational goals and business requirements.
