# Promotion Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a fair, transparent, and merit-based promotion process at Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Promotions recognize employees who consistently demonstrate exceptional performance, professional growth, leadership potential, and meaningful contributions to organizational success.

---

## Eligibility Criteria

Employees may be considered for promotion if they meet the following requirements:

* Minimum 12 months in their current role.
* Consistent performance rating of **"Meets Expectations" (3)** or higher during the most recent review cycle.
* Demonstrated growth in technical, functional, or leadership competencies.
* Successful completion of assigned projects and responsibilities.
* Positive professional conduct and adherence to company policies.

Promotion eligibility does not guarantee promotion.

---

## Promotion Review Cycle

### Annual Promotion Cycle

Promotions are reviewed once per year as part of the annual performance review process.

| Activity                | Timeline           |
| ----------------------- | ------------------ |
| Performance Reviews     | January - February |
| Promotion Assessments   | February           |
| Leadership Approval     | March              |
| Promotion Announcements | April              |

### Exceptional Promotions

In exceptional cases, off-cycle promotions may be approved for employees who demonstrate outstanding contributions, critical business impact, or exceptional leadership capabilities.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Promotion decisions are based on the following factors:

### Performance

* Achievement of goals and objectives
* Quality and consistency of work
* Project delivery and execution
* Business impact

### Technical Competence

* Job-specific expertise
* Problem-solving abilities
* Innovation and continuous improvement
* Adoption of new technologies and practices

### Leadership and Collaboration

* Teamwork and communication
* Mentoring and knowledge sharing
* Initiative and ownership
* Stakeholder management

### Learning and Development

* Certifications and training completion
* Continuous learning efforts
* Professional skill development

---

## Promotion Process

### Step 1: Manager Recommendation

The reporting manager evaluates employee readiness and submits promotion recommendations.

### Step 2: Department Review

Department heads review recommendations against organizational needs, role requirements, and available positions.

### Step 3: HR Review

Human Resources verifies eligibility, performance records, and policy compliance.

### Step 4: Leadership Approval

Final promotion decisions require approval from senior leadership.

### Step 5: Communication

Approved promotions are formally communicated to employees and reflected in HR records.

## Compensation Impact

Promotions may include:

* New job title
* Expanded responsibilities
* Salary adjustment
* Additional leadership opportunities

Compensation changes are determined based on role level, performance, internal equity, and business considerations.

---

## Equal Opportunity

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. promotes equal opportunities for all employees. Promotion decisions are based solely on merit, qualifications, performance, and business requirements.
