# Attendance Policy

## Purpose

This policy outlines attendance expectations, working hours, remote work guidelines, and punctuality standards for all employees of Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

## Working Hours

* Standard working hours are **9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST**, Monday through Friday.
* Employees are expected to complete **8 working hours per day** excluding breaks.
* A lunch break of up to **1 hour** is permitted.

## Attendance Recording

* Employees must record their check-in and check-out through the designated attendance system.
* Attendance records are maintained for payroll, performance evaluation, and compliance purposes.
* Missing attendance entries should be reported to the reporting manager and HR within 24 hours.

## Work From Home (WFH)

* Employees may work remotely based on project requirements and manager approval.
* WFH employees must remain available during standard working hours.
* Participation in scheduled meetings and timely communication is mandatory.
* Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection and a productive work environment.

## Punctuality

* Employees are expected to report to work on time.
* Repeated late arrivals may result in discussions with the reporting manager.
* Frequent attendance irregularities may be considered during performance reviews.

## Absence Reporting

* Planned absences must be requested through the leave management system in advance.
* Unplanned absences should be communicated to the reporting manager as early as possible.
* Extended absences may require supporting documentation.

## Overtime

* Overtime should only be performed when approved by the reporting manager.
* Additional working hours may be required during critical project deliveries.

## Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in corrective action as per company guidelines. HR reserves the right to review attendance records and address any discrepancies when necessary.

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Company:** Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
