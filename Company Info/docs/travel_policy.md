# Travel Policy

## Purpose

This Travel Policy outlines the guidelines for official business travel undertaken by employees of Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. The objective is to ensure that business travel is conducted efficiently, safely, and cost-effectively while supporting organizational goals.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees who undertake domestic or international travel on behalf of the company for purposes such as:

* Client meetings
* Project implementation
* Business development activities
* Conferences and seminars
* Training programs
* Company-sponsored events

---

## Travel Authorization

### Approval Requirements

All business travel must receive prior approval before bookings are made.

| Travel Type          | Approval Authority      |
| -------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Local Travel         | Reporting Manager       |
| Domestic Travel      | Department Head         |
| International Travel | CEO and Department Head |

Travel without proper approval may not be eligible for reimbursement.

---

## Travel Booking Process

### Transportation Booking

Employees should use cost-effective travel options whenever possible.

Approved transportation methods include:

* Air Travel
* Train Travel
* Cab Services
* Public Transportation
* Company-approved travel vendors

### Flight Booking

* Economy class should be selected for all domestic travel.
* Business class requires special approval from senior management.
* Bookings should be made as early as possible to reduce costs.

### Train Travel

Employees are encouraged to use appropriate train classes based on travel duration and business requirements.

---

## Accommodation Guidelines

Employees may stay in company-approved hotels or accommodations during official travel.

Guidelines include:

* Standard business-class accommodation.
* Reasonable room rates based on city and duration.
* Personal upgrades are not reimbursable unless approved.

---

## Daily Allowances

Employees may claim approved expenses for:

* Meals
* Local transportation
* Internet access required for business purposes

Expenses must be supported by valid receipts where applicable.

---

## Expense Reimbursement

Travel-related expenses may be reimbursed in accordance with the Reimbursement Policy.

Eligible expenses include:

* Transportation
* Accommodation
* Business meals
* Client meeting expenses
* Approved incidental expenses

All claims must be submitted within 30 days of travel completion.

---

## Travel Safety

Employees are expected to:

* Follow local laws and regulations.
* Prioritize personal safety during travel.
* Maintain communication with their manager when required.
* Report emergencies or travel disruptions promptly.

The company may provide additional guidance for high-risk travel locations.

---

## International Travel

For international travel, employees must ensure:

* Valid passport and visa documentation.
* Compliance with destination-country regulations.
* Travel insurance coverage where required.
* Approval from senior management prior to travel arrangements.

---

## Personal Travel Extensions

Employees wishing to extend business trips for personal reasons must:

* Obtain prior approval.
* Clearly separate personal and business expenses.
* Bear all costs associated with personal travel arrangements.

The company will only reimburse approved business-related expenses.

---

## Cancellation and Changes

If travel plans change:

* Employees should notify their manager immediately.
* Travel providers should be informed as soon as possible.
* Efforts should be made to minimize cancellation charges and additional costs.

---

## Policy Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in reimbursement denial or disciplinary action where appropriate.

Employees are expected to act responsibly and make prudent financial decisions when traveling on behalf of the company.

---

## Exceptions

Any exception to this policy requires written approval from the relevant Department Head and Human Resources Department.
