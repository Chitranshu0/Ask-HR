# Leave Policy

## Purpose

Feku Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. recognizes the importance of employee well-being and work-life balance. This policy outlines the leave entitlements, eligibility criteria, application process, and carryover rules applicable to all eligible employees.

---

## Leave Categories

### Casual Leave (CL)

Casual Leave is intended for short-term personal commitments, emergencies, or unforeseen situations.

**Entitlement:**

* 12 days per calendar year

**Guidelines:**

* Leave should be applied for in advance whenever possible.
* Casual Leave cannot normally be taken for extended periods exceeding 3 consecutive working days without manager approval.
* Unused Casual Leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

---

### Sick Leave (SL)

Sick Leave is granted when an employee is unable to work due to illness, injury, or medical conditions.

**Entitlement:**

* 10 days per calendar year

**Guidelines:**

* Employees should notify their reporting manager as early as possible.
* Medical documentation may be required for extended sick leave.
* Unused Sick Leave cannot be carried forward and is not encashable.

---

### Earned Leave (EL)

Earned Leave is intended for planned vacations, personal travel, family events, or extended time off.

**Entitlement:**

* 18 days per calendar year

**Guidelines:**

* Leave requests should be submitted at least 7 days in advance whenever possible.
* Approval is subject to project schedules and business requirements.
* Employees are encouraged to coordinate with their managers before planning extended leave.

---

## Carry Forward Rules

### Casual Leave

* Carry Forward: Not Allowed
* Encashment: Not Allowed

### Sick Leave

* Carry Forward: Not Allowed
* Encashment: Not Allowed

### Earned Leave

* Carry Forward: Allowed up to 30 days
* Leave balance exceeding the carry-forward limit will automatically lapse at the end of the leave year unless otherwise approved by HR.

---

## Leave Application Process

1. Submit the leave request through the company leave management system.
2. Provide the leave type, dates, and reason for absence.
3. The reporting manager reviews the request.
4. Upon approval, the leave balance is updated automatically.

Employees should not assume leave is approved until confirmation is received through the system.

---

## Emergency Leave

In case of emergencies:

* Employees should notify their reporting manager as soon as reasonably possible.
* Emergency leave requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
* Supporting documentation may be requested where appropriate.

---

## Public Holidays

Public holidays are separate from annual leave balances and do not reduce an employee's leave entitlement.

The official holiday calendar is published annually by the Human Resources Department.

---

## Leave During Probation

Employees serving a probation period may avail leave subject to manager approval and company guidelines.

Excessive leave during probation may result in probation review or extension where applicable.

---

## Unauthorized Absence

Failure to report to work without notification or approved leave may be treated as unauthorized absence and may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Leave Balance Management

Employees are responsible for monitoring their leave balances through the employee portal and planning leave accordingly.

Managers should ensure fair and consistent leave approvals while maintaining business continuity.

---

## Policy Exceptions

Any exception to this policy requires approval from the Human Resources Department and relevant management authorities.
